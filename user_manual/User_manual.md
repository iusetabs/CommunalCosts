Installation Guide<br>
The app can be downloaded here. After downloading ensure that in settings under security,<br>
allow installation of apps from unknown sources is checked and click on the apk to install the<br>
application.</p>
<p>Starting the App<br>
Locate the app on your home screen and tap on the Communal Costs App icon. This will<br>
start the app, beginning with the welcome screen (see Fig. 1 below). If this is your first time<br>
using the application, click on the sign up button at the bottom of the screen. Otherwise enter<br>
your log in credentials in the fields provided and tap the login button.<br>
<strong>(Figure 1)</strong></p>
<p>Signing Up<br>
After clicking on the sign up button you will be redirected to the sign up screen. Upon<br>
reaching the sign up screen you will be prompted to enter your name, a valid email and a<br>
password. After which tap on the sign up button. (see Fig. 2 below)<br>
<strong>(Figure 2)</strong></p>
<p>Home Collective Screen<br>
After signing in you will be greeted with the home collective view screen. This screen shows<br>
a list of your joined collectives, since youve just created a new account this will be blank.<br>
Tap on the + in the bottom right hand corner to create a new collective. (see Fig. 3 below)<br>
<strong>(Figure 3)</strong></p>
<p>Making a collective<br>
After clicking on the add icon in the bottom right hand corner of the home screen you will be<br>
prompted to either join an existing collective by entering a collective id or creating your own<br>
collective. (See Fig. 4 below)<br>
<strong>(Figure 4)</strong></p>
<p>If you enter a collective id and click join, you will be entered into the collective and sent to the<br>
collectives transaction list. (eg Fig 5 below)<br>
<strong>(Figure 5)</strong></p>
<p>If instead you decide to create a new collective you will be instead redirected to the collective<br>
creation page. Upon which you will be prompted to enter your collectives name, type, id and<br>
its members along with their permission levels. If no permission levels are provided they will<br>
be automatically assigned ordinary member access. When finished click on create to make<br>
the collective and be redirected to the collectives transaction page. (See Fig. 6 below)<br>
<strong>(Figure 6)</strong></p>
<p>Adding a transaction<br>
After creating a collective, transactions can be added by clicking on the + in the bottom right<br>
corner of the transaction view page. (see Fig. 5) After which you will be redirected to the<br>
transaction entry page and prompted to enter the transactions details. (see Fig. 7 below)<br>
<strong>(Figure 7)</strong></p>
<p>Upon entering all the details click the button in the bottom right hand corner to save the<br>
transaction and return to the transaction view screen.</p>
